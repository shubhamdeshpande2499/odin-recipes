Curd Rice

Indian Dish Consumed on lunch or dinner

Made with rice and curd this dish extremely simple and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Curd
  • Cumin seeds
  • Green Chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Peanuts
  • Salt
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Steam cook the rice in a cooker , after it is cooked wait until it cools down
  2. After it cools down , mix curd with the rice
  3. take a pan , put some ghee in it , let it become hot
  4. now put curry leaves , green chillies, peanuts ,cumin seeds and salt and soute it for 2-3 minutes
  5. now put this mixture on the rice and curd mixture
  6. Mix it well
  7. Tada your Delicious curd and rice is ready!!!

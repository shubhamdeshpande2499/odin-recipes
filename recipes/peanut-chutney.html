Peanut Chutney

A delicious and spicy Chutney Made with Peanuts full of nutrients

Made with Peanuts , red chilli powder , salt and Cumin seeds

Ingredients

  • Peanuts(Roasted)
  • Red Chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Cumin Seeds

Tools needed

  • Mortar and pestle

Steps

  1. Put Some Cumin seeds in the mortar and pestle
  2. Crush the seeds until it becomes powder
  3. Now add some peanuts and crush them with the pestle
  4. After crushing for a while add some red chilli powder and salt to taste
  5. Crush for some more minutes
  6. Tada! your Chutney is ready to eat !!
  7. Note-This is the traditional Method of making the chutney , You can also use a mixer grinder

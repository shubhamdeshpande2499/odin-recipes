Maharashtrian Poha

A Maharastrian Dish consumed for breakfast

Made with thin rice flakes, this breakfast food is packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Poha(Thin rice flakes)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Peanuts
  • Curry leaves
  • Green Chilli
  • Onion
  • Cumin seeds
  • Mustard seeds
  • Coriender leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Tumeric powder
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Rinse the poha(thin rice flakes) with water and keep it in a bowl
  2. Heat some cooking oil in a pan
  3. Now put cumin seeds and mustard seeds in them when the oil becomes hot
  4. Wait till the seeds start to pop
  5. Now add chooped onion
  6. Wait until the onion becomes pinkish in color
  7. Now add some salt to taste
  8. Add some tumeric powder
  9. Add green chillies amd curry leaves, soute for 2 mins
  10. Now add the rinsed poha in that pan , cover the pan for 5 mins
  11. Garnish with coriender leaves
  12. Tada your delicious Poha is ready
